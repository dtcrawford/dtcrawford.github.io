Our Story

Founded in 2010 Julio's has become a favorite among everyone in San Diego. Our founder Julio wanted to create a place that was not only cheap but also served quality food. With the help of his friends and family he opend his resturaunt based on these values. Sense the openign the amount of costumers has grown with it.
